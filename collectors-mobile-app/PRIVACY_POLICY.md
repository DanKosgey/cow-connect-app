# Privacy Policy for Cow Connect Collector App

**Last Updated:** January 9, 2026

## Introduction
Cow Connect Collector ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile application designed for dairy milk collectors to record and track milk collections from farmers. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Location Data
- **What:** GPS coordinates (latitude and longitude)
- **Why:** To verify and track milk collection points
- **When:** Only when you record a collection
- **Storage:** Stored locally and synced to our secure servers

### 2. Photos
- **What:** Photos of milk collections
- **Why:** For quality control and record verification
- **When:** Optional - only if you choose to take a photo
- **Storage:** Stored locally until uploaded, then deleted from device

### 3. Collection Data
- **What:** Farmer information, literage, dates, and rates
- **Why:** To maintain accurate milk collection records
- **When:** Every time you record a collection
- **Storage:** Stored in encrypted local database and synced to cloud

### 4. User Account Information
- **What:** Email, name, and phone number
- **Why:** For authentication and account management
- **When:** During registration and login
- **Storage:** Securely stored on Supabase servers

## How We Use Your Information
- Track and verify milk collections
- Calculate earnings and payments
- Generate performance reports
- Ensure data integrity and authenticity
- Improve app functionality

## Data Sharing
We **DO NOT** sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:
- With the dairy cooperative you work for (your employer)
- When required by law
- With your explicit consent

## Data Security
- All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Local data is stored in encrypted SQLite database
- Cloud storage uses industry-standard security (Supabase)
- Access is restricted to authenticated users only

## Your Rights
You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request data deletion
- Withdraw consent for data collection
- Export your data

To exercise these rights, contact your dairy cooperative administrator.

## Children's Privacy
This app is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

## Data Retention
- Active collection data: Retained indefinitely for business records
- Photos: Deleted from device after sync, retained on server for 1 year
- Location data: Retained with collection records
- Account data: Retained while you are employed

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be posted within the app with the updated date.

## Permissions Explained

### Camera Permission
Allows you to take photos of milk collections for documentation purposes. This is optional.

### Location Permission
Required to record GPS coordinates of collection points. This ensures authenticity and helps track collection routes.

### Internet Access
Required to sync your collections to the cloud database when online.
