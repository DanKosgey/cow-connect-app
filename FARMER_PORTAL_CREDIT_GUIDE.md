# Farmer Portal Credit User Guide

## Overview
This guide explains how farmers can use the credit system in the Farmer Portal to request and manage credit against their milk collections.

## Getting Started
1. Log in to the Farmer Portal using your credentials
2. Navigate to the "Credit Dashboard" from the main menu
3. View your current credit status and available balance

## Credit Dashboard

### Credit Summary
- **Available Credit**: The amount of credit you can currently use
- **Credit Limit**: Your maximum credit allowance based on pending collections
- **Credit Tier**: Your status level (New, Established, or Premium)
- **Utilization**: Percentage of your credit limit that you've used

### Recent Activity
- View your 5 most recent credit transactions
- See the status of your pending credit requests

## Requesting Credit

### Step 1: Check Eligibility
- Your eligibility is automatically calculated based on your pending milk collections
- You must have pending collections to be eligible for credit
- Your account must not be frozen

### Step 2: Shop with Credit
1. Click on the "Shop with Credit" tab
2. Browse available agrovet products
3. Add products to your cart
4. Review your cart and total amount
5. Submit your credit request

### Step 3: Track Requests
- View the status of your submitted requests (Pending, Approved, Rejected)
- Receive notifications about request status changes
- Contact admin if you have questions about a request

## Managing Credit

### Viewing Transaction History
1. Click on the "Credit History" tab
2. See all your credit transactions in chronological order
3. Filter by transaction type if needed

### Understanding Credit Tiers
- **New Farmers** (0-3 months): 30% credit against collections, up to KES 50,000
- **Established Farmers** (3-12 months): 60% credit against collections, up to KES 75,000
- **Premium Farmers** (12+ months): 70% credit against collections, up to KES 100,000

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues
- **"Not Eligible for Credit"**: Ensure you have pending milk collections
- **"Insufficient Credit"**: Your request amount exceeds your available balance
- **"Account Frozen"**: Contact admin to resolve account issues

### Contact Support
If you experience any issues with the credit system:
1. Check the "Notifications" section for error messages
2. Contact your cooperative admin for assistance
3. Provide details about the issue including screenshots if possible

## Best Practices
- Regularly check your credit dashboard to monitor your balance
- Submit credit requests early to ensure approval before making purchases
- Keep track of your pending collections as they affect your credit limit
- Use credit responsibly and make repayments through milk collections
